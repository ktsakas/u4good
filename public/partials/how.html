How U4Good works:

You and your friends can set goals and ‘rewards' to spread inspiration and motivation between and around you!

You can either challenge (or support) a friend to accomplish a goal, or you can challenge yourself to become motivated (and your friends can support you in your journey)!

By setting goals, not only you become better, but you are also given the chance to do something Good for the rest of the world!

Oh - and don’t forget to set a deadline!

When YOU challenge a FRIEND:

If they succeed:

  • You offer the ‘gift' to them to congratulate them for their achievement - and then they can choose themselves how to cherish that ‘gift’!
If they fail:
  • You can ask for the ‘gift’ to be returned to you, or
  • You can choose to donate all or part of the ‘gift' to charity!

When YOU challenge YOURSELF:

If you succeed:

  • You can ask for the ‘gift’ to be returned to you, or
  • You can choose to spread the Good by donating all or part of your ‘gift’ to charity!
If you fail:
  • The ‘gift’ is donated to charity; so, even when you “lose,” a person in need somewhere in the world becomes a winner!

How to join:

Sign in through your Facebook account.

Finally, invite your friends to join your journey, and start spreading some Good vibes around!
